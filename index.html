Celebration

At Something Catering, we treat each event with the meticulous care it warrants. Collaborating closely with you at every stage, we infuse each occasion with our natural flair for sophistication and elegance. We excel in hosting upscale cocktails, seated and buffet dinners, weddings, galas, and charity events. Rooted in traditional Zimbabwean cuisine, we tailor every menu to suit your specific needs, ensuring you experience the finest in Zimbabwean hospitality.

Perfect for every event

We’re ready to help you with any event, from baby showers and graduations, to weddings and galas, to corporate cocktails and sit down dinners. We’re deeply committed to making your event unforgettable with fabulous food and impeccable service. Whether you’re planning an intimate dinner at home, or a wedding, we’ll bring our creativity and expertise to your occasion.
